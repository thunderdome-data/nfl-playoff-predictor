How it works: The predictor pairs each of this year's playoff teams with similar teams in each category. Based on which statistics are selected and how they are ranked, the predictor dynamically calculates a percentage chance of each team winning.

Based on the way the predictor operates, a team that fared better in one category than another will not necessarily be more likely to win. (For example, Team A may have passed for fewer yards than Team B, but teams that finished with a similar passing offense to Team A won more often.)

Data is pulled from conference title matchups dating back to the 1971 postseason.
